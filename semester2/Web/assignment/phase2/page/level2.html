Level 2 - The Moon

Location and How to Get There

It's on a small hill in a forest, surrounded by some statues.

The Layout

The name of this level is called Moon.

Its shape represent a crescent moon as well.

Key Items

Item Description Image
The Magical Boomerang

An improved version of the Wooden Boomerang unlocked in level 1. It has a longer range.

The Boss, Dodongo

An orange dinosaur-looking creature.

You need bombs to defeat it, you can either feed it 2 bombs, or stun it with the smoke of a bomb's explosion then finish it off with your sword.

Difficulty

3/10 Easy, the Magical Boomerang room is pretty tough other than that, it's about as hard as the first level.

My Rating

8/10. You could argue that the layout is even more simple compared to level 1.

It has a beautiful dark blue color scheme, with a lot of orange enemies and sand, which it contrasts with very nicely.
