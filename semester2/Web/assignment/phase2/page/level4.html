Level 4 - The Snake

Location and How to Get There

It's found on an island. You need the raft from level 3 to access it.

The Layout

This level is shaped like a snake.

There is a secret room in the snake's eye, you can access with bombs!

Key Items

Item Description Image
The Stepladder

A ladder you can use to cross gaps in water and lava.

The Boss, Gleeok (2 Heads)

A two headed dragon shooting fireballs.

You need to attack its heads to defeat it.

Watch out! Once you destroy a head it gets decapitated and flies around attacking you!

Difficulty

5/10 Slightly easier than level 3 in my opinion.

There are no Darknuts, and the enemies are easier overall.

You can also use the stepladder from now to stay on water tiles where enemies can't go.

My Rating

10/10. My favorite level personally.

the difficulty is just right for me to be the most enjoyable.

The stepladder item will come in very handy throughout the entire game.

I really like the yellow color scheme mixed with blue water.
